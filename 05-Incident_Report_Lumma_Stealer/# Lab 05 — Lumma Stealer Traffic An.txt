# Lab 05 — Lumma Stealer Traffic Analysis

**Tool:** Wireshark  
**Source:** malware-traffic-analysis.net (2026-01-31)  
**Date:** June 2026

## Summary
Analysed a PCAP from a simulated SOC alert for Lumma Stealer 
victim fingerprinting activity. The SIEM alert fired for 
"ET MALWARE Lumma Stealer Victim Fingerprinting Activity" 
triggered by traffic from 153.92.1.49 over TCP port 80.

Lumma Stealer is an information-stealing malware-as-a-service 
(MaaS) capable of harvesting browser credentials, cookies, 
cryptocurrency wallets, and sensitive system data. The infected 
host was identified through HTTP traffic analysis, Ethernet frame 
inspection, and Kerberos authentication records. The C2 domain 
whitepepper.su was identified via the HTTP Host header in 
/api/set_agent fingerprinting calls.

## Findings
- **Infected IP:** 10.1.21.58
- **MAC Address:** 00:21:5d:c8:0e:f2
- **Hostname:** DESKTOP-ES9F3ML
- **User Account:** gwyatt (WIN11OFFICE domain)
- **C2 Domain:** whitepepper.su (153.92.1.49:80)
- **Fingerprinting URI:** /api/set_agent?id=3BF67EC053...

## Wireshark Filters Used
- C2 traffic: `(http.request or tls.handshake.type eq 1) and !(ssdp) and ip.addr eq 153.92.1.49
